LearnEasyWebsite

The LearnEasy E-Learning website was designed to provide an intuitive and engaging user interface for users looking to explore a wide range of online courses. The aim was to create a responsive and visually appealing platform that allows learners to seamlessly navigate through courses, instructor profiles, and learning materials while maintaining the brand’s identity.

Services:
  • UI Design & Development
Client:

LearnEasy

Project link:
www.learneasy.com
Duration:

121 Hours

User-Centric Navigation: The primary objective was to make it easy for users to find and enroll in courses by organizing categories and providing quick access to top-rated classes. Visual Appeal & Consistency: The interface was designed to be clean, with a consistent color palette and typography to reflect professionalism and trustworthiness. Instructor-Centric Features: Special attention was given to instructors, encouraging certified teachers to join the platform with an attractive landing section, explaining the perks and process of becoming an instructor. Responsiveness: The design ensures that the platform functions flawlessly across devices, making it accessible from desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Interactive Elements: Engaging sections like testimonials, popular courses, and course details were designed to improve user engagement
