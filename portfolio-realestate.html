RealEasy-Real Estate App

RealEasy is a mobile-first real estate platform designed to simplify the property search and home-buying experience. The objective was to create a clean, accessible interface that empowers users to discover, evaluate, and connect with properties and agents in just a few taps.

Services:
  • UX/UI Designer & Visual Strategist
Client:

RealEasy (Concept Project)

Platform:

iOS & Android

Tools:

Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop


Project Background

The real estate experience is often stressful and fragmented, especially when searching for a home via mobile apps. RealEasy was conceptualized as a solution to simplify property discovery, build trust with verified agents, and streamline the buying process — all from a smartphone. My role spanned from ideation to final screen design. I collaborated closely with stakeholders to ensure that user pain points were addressed with clear UX logic and modern UI aesthetics.

Design Goals

To design an intuitive, trust-driven real estate platform that supports smooth user onboarding, transparent listings, and quick access to verified agents.

User Research & Empathy Mapping

We identified two main personas:

Buyers looking for clarity, speed, and legitimacy

Agents seeking trust, visibility, and quick lead connections

Key Pain Points Identified:

Confusing filters on competitor apps

Lack of verified agent credibility

Poor mobile responsiveness and flow disruptions

Information overload in search results

Solutions Provided

Smart Filtering with clear UI and customizable search criteria

Agent Verification & Reviews to build trust

Saved Searches & Alerts for user convenience

Map Integration with list-view toggle

Simple, Guided Onboarding

Polished Visual Hierarchy with modern UI and strong UX writing
