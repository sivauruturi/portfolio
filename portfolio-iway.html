Project Background

IWAY Infotech is a technology-focused company aiming to establish a strong, trustworthy presence in the digital space. The team approached me to create a clean, bold, and modern logo that reflects both innovation and clarity in their brand communication.

Design Goals

Create a timeless, professional identity that works across digital and print formats

Emphasize tech-forward values without overcomplicating the visual style

Deliver a logo that supports scalability, legibility, and minimalism

Concept & Approach

The wordmark “IWAY” was designed using custom letterforms with a modern, geometric influence. Each letter was crafted to maintain strong alignment and visual rhythm, subtly expressing precision and connectivity—hallmarks of a forward-looking tech company.

I opted for a bold, high-contrast monochrome color palette to ensure versatility across media. Whether placed on light, dark, or branded backgrounds, the identity remains consistent and recognizable.
